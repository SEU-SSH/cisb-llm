LLVM’s ArgumentPromotion optimization at -O2 caused s390 sock_fields test failure. The optimization promotes function arguments, inadvertently transforming a 2-byte load of dst_port into a 4-byte load. This discrepancy arises because the current uapi bpf_sock structure defines dst_port as a 16-bit field with padding, unlike older kernels where it was a 32-bit field. The workaround, inserting an empty asm volatile, prevents this unwanted transformation, ensuring correct behavior even if the LLVM patch is reinstated.
